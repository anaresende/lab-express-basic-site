Did you know ?

Arya Stark is without a doubt the most bad-ass character of the GoT and ASoIaF worlds.

Arya rejects the notion that she must become a lady and marry for influence and power. Instead, she believes that she can forge her own destiny. She is fascinated by warfare and training in the use of arms, and is bored by embroidery and other "lady-like" pursuits.
